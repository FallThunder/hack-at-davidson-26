What it does

Everything you need to read critically

Evident runs in the background and surfaces trust signals the moment you open a news article.

Trust Score

A single 0–100 score weighing publisher credibility, political neutrality, content factuality, and the severity of flagged claims.

Site Profile

Political bias bar and factual reporting rating sourced from Media Bias/Fact Check via live AI web search — shown for every article.

Inline Highlights

Questionable sentences highlighted directly on the article — yellow for minor framing, orange for misleading claims, red for false ones.

Flag Analysis

Each flagged claim shows a confidence percentage, detailed reasoning, and source links — click any flag in the panel to jump to it on the page.

Accessibility Audit

Live DOM scan that checks heading structure, image alt text, link text quality, and labeling — scored 0–100 with per-issue breakdown.

Audio Summary

Hit play and Evident reads a concise spoken summary of the trust analysis — generated by Gemini and synthesized by ElevenLabs.

How it works

Analysis in three steps

  1. 1

    Install the extension

    Download the zip for Chrome or Firefox, then load it as an unpacked extension or temporary add-on. No account required.

  2. 2

    Navigate to any news article

    Evident activates on supported news sites. Click the toolbar icon to open the analysis panel alongside the article.

  3. 3

    Read the results

    The trust score, site profile, and flagged claims appear within seconds. Highlights are applied directly to the text you are reading.
